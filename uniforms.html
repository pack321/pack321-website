Pack 321 Uniform Expectations

Uniforms help Scouts feel like part of the Pack and make ceremonies, photos, and meetings feel special. Families do not need everything on day one. Start with the essentials for your Scout's den and ask a leader if you are unsure.

Official Uniform Guide

Pack 321 Tip

If cost or sizing is a concern, please ask a Pack leader. We want every Scout included and comfortable, and we can help families figure out the best next step.

Ask A Question

When To Wear It

  • Pack meetings and den meetings
  • Flag ceremonies and community service
  • Blue & Gold, graduation, and award nights
  • Public events where Scouts represent Pack 321
  • Bottoms: Scouts may wear khaki pants or shorts, jeans, or skorts. Clothing should be clean, comfortable, and appropriate for Scouting activities. Please avoid jogging pants, sweatpants, athletic shorts, leggings worn as pants, and pajama-style bottoms.
  • Footwear: Closed-toe shoes are required for all Pack meetings and activities. For safety, sandals, flip-flops, Crocs without the heel strap secured, and other open-toed footwear are not permitted.
