Why Pack 321

A family-first Pack built around belonging, adventure, character, organization, and camaraderie.

👨‍👩‍👧‍👦

Family First

Parents participate, siblings attend, grandparents are welcome, and no one experiences the journey alone.

🌎

Everyone Belongs

Boys and girls from any school or community are welcome. Every family has something valuable to contribute.

📋

Organized & Helpful

Families should feel informed, supported, and confident about what comes next.

Our promise

Pack 321 exists so that every family has a place to belong, every Scout has opportunities to grow, and every adventure becomes a lasting memory.

What success looks like

Success is not just the number of events we host. It is the character, skills, friendships, and memories Scouts carry long after Cub Scouts.
