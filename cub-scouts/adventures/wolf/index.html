Wolf Adventures

Wolf Adventures give second grade Scouts practical ways to build skills, confidence, fitness, safety, service, and outdoor curiosity.

Required Adventures

Character & Leadership Bobcat Adventure Build den belonging and learn the first safety and family foundations. Personal Fitness Running With the Pack Explore healthy choices, movement, rest, and personal wellness. Personal Safety Safety in Numbers Practice safety awareness and trusted-adult communication. Family & Reverence Footsteps Explore reverence, gratitude, and family values. Citizenship Council Fire Learn about community, service, and doing your part. Outdoors Paws on the Path Practice outdoor skills, hiking awareness, and preparedness.
