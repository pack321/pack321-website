Lion Adventures

Lion Adventures are fun, hands-on activities that help Kindergarten children learn new things, build confidence, and grow a love for learning and the outdoors.

Required Adventures

Character & Leadership Bobcat Adventure ↗ Build den belonging and learn the first safety and family foundations. Personal Fitness Fun on the Run ↗ Practice active play, healthy habits, and taking care of your body. Personal Safety Lion's Roar ↗ Learn simple safety steps and how to ask trusted adults for help. Family & Reverence Lion's Pride ↗ Explore family, gratitude, and reverence in age-appropriate ways. Citizenship King of the Jungle ↗ Practice helping the group, using good manners, and being kind. Outdoors Mountain Lion ↗ Get outside, explore nature, and learn basic outdoor awareness.
