Required Adventures

Character & Leadership Bobcat Adventure ↗ Build den belonging and learn the first safety and family foundations. Personal Fitness Stronger, Faster, Higher ↗ Build fitness habits and learn how your body grows stronger. Personal Safety My Safety ↗ Practice safety planning and trusted-adult communication. Family & Reverence My Family ↗ Explore family identity, respect, and reverence. Citizenship My Community ↗ Learn how communities work and how Scouts can help. Outdoors Webelos Walkabout ↗ Prepare for and complete an outdoor walkabout.
