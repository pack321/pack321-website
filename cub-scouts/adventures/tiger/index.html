Tiger Adventures

Tiger Adventures help first grade Scouts learn by doing with their adult partners, their den, and their pack.

Required Adventures

Character & Leadership Bobcat Adventure Build den belonging and learn the first safety and family foundations. Personal Fitness Tiger Bites Practice nutrition, movement, and personal wellness. Personal Safety Tiger's Roar Learn safety habits and how to respond when something feels unsafe. Family & Reverence Tiger Circles Talk about family, gratitude, and reverence with trusted adults. Citizenship Team Tiger Practice teamwork, service, and being part of a community. Outdoors Tigers in the Wild Explore the outdoors and learn simple outdoor skills.
