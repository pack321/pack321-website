Pack Leadership

Meet the Team

Pack 321 is led by volunteers who care about creating a welcoming, organized, and fun Scouting experience for every family.

Leadership With Heart

Our leaders help plan meetings, guide adventures, communicate with families, and keep the Pack moving together.

CM

Cubmaster

Leads Pack meetings, keeps the energy high, and helps Scouts feel seen and celebrated.

DL

Den Leaders

Guide Scouts by grade level through adventures, activities, rank progress, and friendship.

CC

Pack Committee

Supports planning, fundraising, events, communication, and the behind-the-scenes work that makes Pack 321 possible.

Want to Help?

You do not need to know everything about Scouting to volunteer. Pack 321 has room for planners, helpers, encouragers, builders, communicators, and friendly extra hands.
