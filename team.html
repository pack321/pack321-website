The People Behind the Adventure

Pack 321 is built by leaders, den leaders, parents, volunteers, and families working together.

Shared leadership

The Committee Chair helps organize and direct what needs to happen, but Pack 321 succeeds through the guidance and effort of Cubmasters, den leaders, committee members, parents, and volunteers.

It takes a Pack

Every family has something valuable to contribute. Some lead dens. Some help events. Some set up chairs. All of it matters.

Committee Chair

Organizes, coordinates, and helps align the Pack direction.

Cubmaster

Leads Pack meetings and helps bring the Cub Scout program to life.

Den Leaders

Guide Scouts through adventures, advancement, and age-level activities.
